About

Hi again, I'm Dylan Blanchard. I'm hopelessly curious, hate shitty experiences, and love the outdoors.

At heart, I'm a researcher who's not satisfied just doing the research but needs to ensure we're solving problems too. Most energized by big, tangled problems that affect a lot of people. If I'm not digging into problems, I'm most likey asking friends to teach me an idea they recently read about.

I love biking, hiking, pizza, mountains, and the ocean.

Work

w

I help lead the Research team at Shopify where we do really dope research.

Research is hands-down the most interesting job and I love it. Working on understanding the vast, varied, and (sometimes) complex workflows of entrepreneurs around the globe has taught me to take a systems approach to research, employing open eyes and ears to understand the world we live in. I'm also building out a team of world-class researchers hooked on supporting our entrepreneurs. You can read about our work here: Shopify UX Blog and explore open roles on my team here: Shopify Careers.
